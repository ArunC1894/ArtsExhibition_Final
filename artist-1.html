MICHAEL QUANE RHA




Michael Quane Rha born is Coachford, Co Cork and he won McKeon Stone Award at sculpture in context ,2022 for his work Aqualung Buoyancy which made
it on Kilkenny limestone. Michael Quane graduated from the Crawford School of Art and Design in Cork in 1987. He was elected a member of Aosdána
in 1998 and a full member of the Royal Hibernian Academy in 2004.In 1987, Michael Quane received his diploma from the Crawford School of Art and Design
in Cork. In 1998, he was elected to the Aosdána, and in 2004, he was made a full member of the Royal Hibernian Academy.
Horses and Riders on the Mallow highway junction, The Predator on the Kilkenny canal path,
and Person Talking to a Quadruped in University College Cork are only a few of his numerous public works.
His art is included in both private and public collections, and he has exhibited in Ireland, the UK, Western Europe, and the US.
