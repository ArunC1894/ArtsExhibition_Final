Sculptures of MICHAEL QUANE RHA
Buoyed
56 x 46 x 20cm

Carrara marble & welded steel plinth, unique Buoyed is a part of family of sculpture and it means balance, equilibrium, gravity and levity kneaded into one. This sculpture work started on 2019 on Italian marble and it has reworked until 2020.

AQUALUNG BUOYANCY
56 x 46 x 19cm

This work has won McKeon Stone Award at sculpture in the context, 2022. This sculpture was initially named as Making Buoyancy and later it changed to Aqualung Buoyancy in 2018. This also a part of Buoyed family.

SYNC
200 x 170 x 50cm (Bronze)
45 x 50 x 50cm (Silver)

This works may serve as an expression of the idea that gravity serves as a substitute for an atheistic God.
