Jack B Yeats




Jack B Yeats was a very successful 20th century Irish artist. He first worked as a graphic designer and cartoonist.
However, he is best known for his paintings of everyday life in Ireland and Celtic myths. Yeats was born in 1871 in London.
His family is very famous. His father was the Irish portrait painter John Butler Yeats. Poet William Butler Yeats was his brother.
Her two older sisters are also famous artists, Elizabeth Corbet Yeats, and Susan Mary Yeats.
